06 / THE PROCESS

Four steps.
From quote to handover.

  1. 01

    Site Inspection

    Free on-site walk-through. We assess access, services, hazards, disposal route, and ask the questions that surface hidden costs before they become invoices.

  2. 02

    Fixed-Scope Quote

    Written quote inside 24 hours. Clear scope of works, inclusions, exclusions, timing, and the exact price you'll pay — no fine print, no day-rates that blow out.

  3. 03

    Demolition & Excavation

    Crew and machinery on-site to schedule. Disconnects, permits, dust & noise control, recycling — handled. We work to your builder's program, not ours.

  4. 04

    Clean Handover

    Site graded, swept, photographed, and signed off. Levelled, compacted, ready for footings. Builders love an Apex finish — fewer surprises on day one.
